Planting Notes

Height flows tall → short, back to front, so nothing gets shaded out. Sunflower seed heads left standing in fall will feed goldfinches and cardinals through the birdhouse. The trumpet vine on the fence keeps it from competing for bed space — but prune it yearly. Scatter the wildflower mix anywhere you see bare soil between plants. Direct-sow everything after mid-March (last frost).
